The polity_speeches folder includes all scraped documents from "https://www.polity.org.za/page/speeches/" on 28/07/2019. File name schemas are as follows:
d-Month-YYYY - Title of Speech (i.e. "1st April 2016  - DBE Angie Motshekga Address by Minister of Basic Education at the Minister s meeting with district directors Sol Plaatje house Pretoria 31 03 2016 ")

Titles with illegal characters have been removed and only include the date of the document.
Each document containts all text <p> elements in the content block of the article seperated by \n each.

NOTE: The <p> tags also scraped add text which means the text "Advertisement" appears a couple of times in between the content paragraphs.
