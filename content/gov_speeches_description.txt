The gov_speeches folder includes all scraped documents from "https://www.gov.za/speeches" on 28/07/2019. File name schemas are as follows:
d-MMM-YYYY - Title of Speech (i.e. "1-Apr-2011 - Budget vote address for the Free State Department of Education by the Honourable MEC for Education Mr PHI Makgoe Free State Provincial Legislature Hertzogville")

Titles with illegal characters have been removed and only include the date of the document.
Each document containts all text <p> elements in the content block of the article seperated by \n each.
